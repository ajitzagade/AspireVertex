Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About
Optima Aspire

Optima Aspire (Survey No. 28/1/6) offers 2 & 3 BHK apartments starting ₹72 Lakhs with premium vitrified flooring, modular kitchen provisions and standard bathroom fittings. Optima Aspire Plus (Survey No. 28/9) offers larger 2.5 & 3 BHK apartments starting ₹95 Lakhs with Italian marble flooring, designer modular kitchen, jacuzzi provision in master bath, and superior overall specifications. Both phases share the same clubhouse, infinity pool and amenity infrastructure.

Optima Aspire is on track for possession in Q2 2026 (April–June 2026). Optima Aspire Plus is scheduled for Q3 2026 (July–September 2026). Construction is currently ahead of schedule — all wings have reached the 12th floor slab as of May 2025. Aspire Group has a 100% on-time delivery record across all past projects.

Yes. Both Optima Aspire and Optima Aspire Plus are registered under MahaRERA with Registration No. P52100047822. All statutory approvals including Building Plan Approval, Pune Municipal Corporation NOC, Environmental Clearance, Fire NOC, and Tree Authority permission are in place. Details are publicly accessible on maharerait.mahaonline.gov.in.

We offer 3 flexible payment plans for Optima Aspire: (1) Construction-Linked Plan — pay as construction progresses with fixed milestones. (2) Down-Payment Advantage Plan — pay 20% now and the balance at possession with attractive discounts. (3) Subvention Plan — select banks offer EMI moratorium until possession. Our team will explain each plan and advise the best option based on your financial situation. Home loans available from SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak at competitive rates.

Optima Aspire is located approximately 2.5 km from the Katraj–Dehu Road Bypass (NH-48), providing excellent connectivity to both Pune city (12 km) and the Pune–Bengaluru Highway for outstation travel. The NH-48 expansion project is expected to further reduce commute times. Swargate bus terminal is 7.8 km away and Pune Railway Station is 12 km.
