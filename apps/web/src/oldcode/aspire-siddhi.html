Project Overview

Life Elevated at
Katraj

Siddhi Aspire is Aspire Group's flagship residential development in Katraj — a vibrant, rapidly growing neighbourhood with excellent connectivity to Pune's major IT corridors, educational institutions and healthcare facilities. The project offers 1 to 4 BHK residences across 7 towers with premium amenities and vastu-compliant layouts.

Every apartment is designed with cross-ventilation, optimum natural light, premium vitrified tile flooring, modular kitchen provisions, full-height windows and branded bathroom fittings. The development integrates 40% open green spaces with a rooftop infinity pool and a 12,000 sq.ft. clubhouse.
